Stress & Burnout

Nursing burnout is physical, mental, and emotional exhaustion caused by chronic workplace stress. It is not your fault, and you are not alone.

Common Signs:

  • Dreading going to clinicals/work.
  • Feeling irritable or cynical with patients.
  • Physical fatigue even after sleeping.

Immediate Action:

The "3-Minute Reset": Step into a supply room or break room. Drink a glass of water slowly. Do one cycle of box breathing. Remind yourself: "I am doing my best in a broken system."

Exam Anxiety

The NCLEX and nursing finals are designed to test critical thinking, not just memory. Anxiety can block that critical thinking pathway.

The Stop Light Method:

  • Green: You know the answer. Mark it, move on.
  • Yellow: You can narrow it down to two. Flag it (if allowed) or pick your gut instinct.
  • Red: No idea. Do not panic. Breathe. Guess C (or your strategy). Move on.
