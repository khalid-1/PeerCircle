Stress & Burnout

Nursing burnout is physical, mental, and emotional exhaustion caused by chronic workplace stress. It is not your fault.

Common Signs

  • Dreading clinicals/work.
  • Feeling cynical with patients.
  • Physical fatigue after sleeping.

Immediate Action: The "3-Minute Reset"

Step into a supply room. Drink water slowly. Do one cycle of box breathing. Remind yourself: "I am doing my best."

Exam Anxiety

The NCLEX tests critical thinking, not just memory. Anxiety blocks that pathway.

The Stop Light Method

  • Green: Know it? Mark it, move on.
  • Yellow: Narrowed to two? Pick your gut.
  • Red: No idea? Don't panic. Guess. Move on.
