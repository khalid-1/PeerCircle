Understanding Burnout

What is it? Nursing burnout is physical, mental, and emotional exhaustion. It causes a detachment from patients and a lack of accomplishment.

The 3 Signs:

  • Exhaustion: Feeling tired even after sleeping.
  • Depersonalization: Feeling cynical towards patients.
  • Inefficacy: Feeling like your work doesn't matter.

Tip: Don't skip your breaks. Even 10 minutes off the floor resets your cortisol levels.

Conquering Exam Anxiety

Nursing exams often require critical thinking, not just memorization. This causes high stress.

Strategy: The "Stop Light" Method

  • Green: Answer questions you know immediately.
  • Yellow: Mark questions you need to think about for later.
  • Red: Questions you don't know. Don't panic, guess intelligently at the end.
